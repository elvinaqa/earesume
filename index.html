The Rise of Coronavirus Action Unity Game

 Game Development

The Rise of Coronavirus game is build in Unity Engine with the help of C# language and its features and libraries for gaming and app development.
As a team of 3 devs, we started to design, code, and build the game from zero to working MVP in 1 day during the GlobalGameJam competition.
The scope and main theme of the game is about:
More than 15K people all over the globe has been infected by Coronavirus recently which shocked whole world making everyone fall into fear of catching it. As a team of young game developers, we created a game to fight virtually against Corona, especially for the people who have been locked down in 13 cities, especially Wuhan. Main charactecter is the saver of the whole country determined to heal everyone he comes accross. The saver is the expert doctor who has found the vaccine for the disease so that he tries his best to save everyone besides his daughter is in the affected city as well.

AI based Review Summarizer for E-Commerce

 AI Application Development with Python Django

We builded an AI application, specifically an NLP project for online shopping websites which summarizes customer reviews of pages long to the much smaller amount,thus, enabling users to not lose time on reading them. After the reviews is scraped from the Amazon website (can be Ebay, Aliexpress, Shoppify as well), raw and meaningful material is extracted. Then, fake and susceptible reviews are also removed from the tank of reviews while only needed ones with more weight is preserved.Used technologies include Sraping libraries, Scrapy, BS4, Sentiment Analysis and Polarity calculators, and BoW methods of NLP
The website enabled us to keep up with stats and upload images, videos and so on to.
